Why This Matters

The Circadian Rhythm

Your body has a natural 24-hour internal clock. Light, especially blue light from screens, signals to your brain that it's daytime.

Evening Screen Time

Exposure to bright, cool-toned light at night can disrupt this clock, making it harder to fall asleep and reducing overall sleep quality.

How We Help

This extension gradually reduces screen brightness and adds a warm tint in the evening, mimicking a natural sunset to help your body wind down for a better night's rest.
