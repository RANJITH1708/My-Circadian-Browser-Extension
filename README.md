# My-Circadian-Browser-Extension
Automatically adjusts your screen's brightness and warmth throughout the day to match your natural sleep-wake cycle. As your bedtime approaches, it gradually dims your screen and warms it with an amber tint, reducing blue light to help your body wind down naturally for a healthier, more restful night's sleep.
